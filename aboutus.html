About Me

S.K Lodge is ideally located in the heart of the village Anaimalai near Masani Amman Kovil and very close to the bus stop. The Lodge gives you a happiest stay, whether you are a seasoned traveller or just a visitor wanting to discover the heritage sites of Anaimalai. S.K lodge is a ideal place to stay. After a long day out, step into the comforts of your spacious and well designed lodge room. The lodge rooms are designed well with all necessary room facilities.


How to get S.K LODGE

By Train:
Coimbatore Junction is the nearest major railway station(55 KM) from Anaimalai. Pollachi junction (21 KM) from Anaimalai.

By Bus:
Anaimalai is well connected to pollachi and coimbatore by government and private buses. The busfrequency is about 5-10 Minutes.

By Air:
Coimbatore Airport is the nearest airport(70 KM) from Anaimalai.
